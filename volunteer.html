Join Our Volunteer Team!

Help us keep our shelter running and provide love and care to animals in need. Your time and dedication make a huge difference!

Why We Need Volunteers

We can always use volunteers to help keep our shelter open! Volunteers are needed to bathe and groom animals, play with pets to help them socialize and keep them happy, clean kennels and screening rooms, and assist with administrative tasks in the office. If you’re interested in volunteering, please complete the steps below.

How to Become a Volunteer

  1. Submit a message of interest to volunteer@welovepets.com.
  2. Once you get a reply, fill out the form given.
  3. Participate in a brief phone interview.
  4. Attend a volunteer training session on the first Saturday of every month.

Requirements to Be a Volunteer

  • Must be at least 18 years old (or 16 with parental consent).
  • Ability to commit to at least 5 hours per month.
  • Comfortable working with animals of all sizes.
  • Willingness to follow shelter safety guidelines and protocols.
  • Attend regular training sessions as needed.
